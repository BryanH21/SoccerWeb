Free Clinics

Community sessions to experience Foundry’s training style: upbeat, fundamentals first, and fun. Perfect for new families.

What to Expect

Our free clinics let players and families try Foundry without any pressure. Sessions emphasize ball control, decision making games, and a positive environment.

  • When: Occasional dates throughout the year
  • Where: NUSC Fields in Noblesville, IN (exact field announced per session)
  • Who: Players ages 8–14, all skill levels welcome!
  • Cost: Always free

Spots are limited and filled first come, first served once dates are announced.
