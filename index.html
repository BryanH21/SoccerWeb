What Results Can You Expect?

At Foundry Soccer Academy, we focus on real results. Parents can expect their children to develop critical in-game technical skills and improved soccer intelligence. Our players consistently gain confidence and creativity through structured drills and hands-on coaching.

Game-Ready Moves

Master advanced footwork like croqueta, v-cuts, scissors, and inside-out cuts to beat defenders in 1v1s.

First Touch Control

Develop first-touch skills that allow players to keep possession and transition into attacking play.

1v1 Confidence

Learn how to take on defenders with purpose and creativity in real match scenarios.

Agility & Awareness

Improve reaction time and positioning with soccer-specific agility and spatial awareness training.

Composure & Decision-Making

Gain composure under pressure and make better decisions during high-stakes moments.

About the Coach

Coach Bryan

I am passionate about developing youth players and watching them reach their potential through my program. I played soccer for many years before I became a coach and through my experiences as a player I aim to coach the new generation properly and avoid the mistakes I made. I have been a head coach for many teams over the past 2 years and have had several additional training sessions with NUSC's Rec+ training program. I am certified through US and Indiana Soccer along with being grassroots licensed.

What Makes Foundry Soccer Academy Unique?

At Foundry Soccer Academy, we go beyond the standard training. Our mission is to forge well-rounded athletes by emphasizing core values such as discipline, leadership, and personal growth. With a curriculum backed by certified coaching, small group attention, and individualized progress tracking, each player receives the support they need to thrive.

We invest in your child’s development both on and off the field—focusing not just on technical skills, but on building confidence, responsibility, and a winning mindset. Choosing Foundry means choosing a program that values every player's long-term success.
Foundry Soccer Academy proudly serves families in Noblesville, Fishers, Carmel, Westfield, and across Hamilton County. Looking for proven private soccer coaching?
