Improve Vital Skills

Ball Control

A key component to every player's game, without good ball control players lack the ability to perform basic movements in soccer such as dribbling, first touch, and precision passing.

Shooting

Goalscoring is the name of the game, if you cant finish your chances you lose the game! Learn how to strike the ball, increase power in your shots, and improve your accuracy.

Defending

While scoring goals is important so is keeping them out, learn to defend by being patient and identifying when to go for the ball.

Benefits of Group Soccer Training

Teamwork & Communication

Group Training enhances teamwork and communication, crucial skills for any soccer player.

Game-Like Scenarios

Practicing in a group setting simulates real-game situations, helping players adapt to pressure.

Competitive Environment

Training with others fosters a competitive atmosphere that pushes players to improve.

Motivation & Support

Training with peers provides motivation and support, making the learning process more enjoyable.

What Makes Foundry Soccer Academy Unique?

At Foundry Soccer Academy, we go beyond the standard training. Our mission is to forge well-rounded athletes by emphasizing core values such as discipline, leadership, and personal growth. With a curriculum backed by certified coaching, small group attention, and individualized progress tracking, each player receives the support they need to thrive.

We invest in your child’s development both on and off the field—focusing not just on technical skills, but on building confidence, responsibility, and a winning mindset. Choosing Foundry means choosing a program that values every player's long-term success.
