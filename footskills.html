Friday Night Foot Skills

Sharpen ball control, build technical confidence, and unleash creativity! All in a high energy, fun environment.

Program Details

Friday Night Foot Skills is a special training program offered in partnership between Foundry Soccer Academy and Noblesville United Soccer Club (NUSC). Designed to sharpen players’ ball control, technical confidence, and creativity on the field. Foot Skills provides focused, high energy training in a fun and challenging environment.

  • When: Friday evenings during the Fall and Spring seasons
  • Where: NUSC Fields (Noblesville, IN)
  • Cost: $75 per season
  • Who Can Join: Open to NUSC players and community players ages 8–14
  • How to Register: Registration is handled directly through NUSC’s website and sign up portal

Available during outdoor club seasons (Spring & Fall). Spots are limited and filled on a first come, first serve basis through NUSC registration.
