Seasonal Camps

Camps combine high energy on field training with structured at home accountability. Each camp runs for five days, featuring three in person sessions (2 hours each) and two guided at home assignments focused on skill development and personal reflection.

Camp Overview

Foundry seasonal camps deliver a mix of training intensity and fun. Sessions are designed to sharpen skills, improve soccer IQ, and build competitive confidence in a supportive environment.

  • When: Offered during summer and school breaks
  • Where: NUSC Fields in Noblesville, IN (field locations announced before each camp)
  • Who: Players ages 8–14, MAX 100 players
  • Cost: $150

Spots are limited and filled first come, first served. Early registration recommended.
